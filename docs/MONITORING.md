# Monitoring Plan & Rapid-Check Prompt

**Purpose:** quick checklist and copy-paste prompts you can run every 30–60 minutes to verify critical changes (VicEmergency, BOM wind change, road closures).

Quick checklist (30–60 minute cadence)
- VicEmergency: check advice level for Bendigo / Epsom (search "Bendigo" on emergency.vic.gov.au)
- ArcGIS / Incident feed: confirm incidents loaded in the app and note last-update timestamp
- BOM: check forecast + warnings; note wind change timing (hour)
- Road closures: ABC Central Vic or VicTraffic for Calder Hwy / Midland Hwy
- Radio: keep ABC Central Victoria tuned for local emergency broadcasts

Rapid-check prompt (copy/paste into any browser-capable LLM or agent):

"List any VicEmergency incidents within 200 km of 53 Buckland St, Epsom VIC. For each, show advice level, last-updated time, and whether it's 'Not Yet Under Control' or 'Under Control'. Also report BOM forecast wind change for Bendigo in next 12 hours (direction & approximate time)."

What to do if something changes
- Advice level upgraded to 'Watch & Act' for Bendigo/Epsom → prepare to leave within 30–60 mins; pack car and essential items now
- Advice level upgraded to 'Emergency Warning' for Bendigo/Epsom → leave immediately following VicEmergency instructions
- BOM wind change arrives earlier than forecast → re-evaluate departure time and route immediately

Notes
- Keep this silent and short — one person monitors while others rest.
- Use the app's manual refresh button before trusting the map readings.
