# Incident Dictionary — Quick Definitions

Emergency Warning: Immediate danger — leave now or take shelter. Use VicEmergency advice and follow official instructions.

Watch & Act: Be ready to leave. Conditions are changing — prepare now and monitor updates.

Advice: Stay informed. No immediate action required but monitor official sources.

Not Yet Under Control / Being Controlled / Contained: Terms used by incident controllers; "Not Yet Under Control" indicates active spread risk.

Spotting: Embers carried ahead of the fire, causing new ignitions. Raises ember attack risk.

Crowning: Fire burning through tree canopy — can cause rapid, fast-moving fire behaviour.

Ember Attack: When burning embers land on structures or vegetation and start new fires.
