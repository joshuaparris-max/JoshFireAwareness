When to ACT

LEAVE immediately if:
- VicEmergency: Watch & Act or Emergency Warning
- You see flames or heavy smoke
- Emergency Alert SMS arrives
- ABC Radio announces evacuation
- Your gut says "not safe"

If in doubt, leave early.

  • Check VicEmergency before leaving
  • Full tank of fuel

  • Car seats checked and secured
  • Phone fully charged + car charger
  • ABC Radio tuned in while driving
  • If smoke heavy: pull over, close windows, check VicEmergency
